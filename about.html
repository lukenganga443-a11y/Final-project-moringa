About RecipeVault

RecipeVault is a simple recipe web application designed to help users explore meals from around the world, save favorite dishes, and discover new cooking ideas easily.

Why Food Matters

Food brings people together and connects cultures. Every country has unique dishes that represent traditions, history, and creativity. Trying foods from different places helps people learn more about the world and experience different lifestyles through cooking.
